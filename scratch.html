MY MISSION

I believe that photography is more than just capturing images. It's about capturing emotions, preserving memories, and bringing eternal peace and happiness to those who view them. Every photograph tells a story, and through my lens, I strive to tell stories that inspire, comfort, and bring joy to the world.

My journey combines three powerful passions: art, creativity, and marketing. This unique blend allows me to not only create beautiful, meaningful photographs but also to help others pursue their dreams through visual storytelling and strategic communication.

MY PUBLISHED WORK

Take a look at the two Ke Alaka'i Fall issues I contributed to as a photographer!

Back in 2022, I studied at BYU-Hawaii and worked for Ke Alaka'i as a photographer and part of the social media team. Ke Alaka'i is one of my favorite workplaces because everyone worked with unity and team spirit, which made it different from anywhere else.

I was able to build connections with students, faculty, staff, and the special community in Lā'ie, O'ahu. Through photography, I connected closely with people facing different challenges - being a mother, being an athlete, dealing with mental struggles, and more. Everyone has a story to tell, and their stories are worth sharing.
